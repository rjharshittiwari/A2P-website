Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Visit our Registration page, fill in your details, select your preferred course, and complete the enrollment process. Our admissions team will guide you through.

What are the course fees?

Course fees vary depending on the course level and duration. Please contact our admissions team or visit the course page for specific pricing information.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes! We offer both online and offline courses. You can choose the learning mode that best suits your schedule and preferences.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% refund if you withdraw within 7 days of enrollment. Please contact support for more details.

Is there placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement assistance including interview preparation, resume building, and job placement help.

How long are courses?

Course duration varies from 4 weeks to 16 weeks depending on the program. Details are available on each course page.
